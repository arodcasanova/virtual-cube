Rubik's Cube VR

Spacebar: Return to white center piece.

--- - - - ---

'A' and 'D' Keys: Toggle left and right through cube cells.

'W' and 'S' Keys: Toggle up and down through cube cells.

--- - - - ---

NOTE: If you hear the error message "Out of bounds", the cube cannot rotate in that direction. Hit Spacebar to return to white center piece.
